Practice Helper – Your All‑in‑One Practice Management Hub
=============================================================

**A sleek, single‑pane SaaS solution for accounting and finance firms** that centralises clients, staff, tasks, and audit/compliance activities.

### Core Features
- **Client Management** – Create, edit, and organise client profiles, contacts, and engagement history.
- **Staff Management** – Add staff, assign roles (Partner, Manager, Staff), and view workload snapshots.
- **Task & Workload Scheduling** – Create tasks, set deadlines, assign to staff, and track progress via an intuitive board.
- **Auto‑Task Generation** – Generate multiple tasks based on client‑staff relations and compliance periods (monthly, quarterly, yearly) to save time.
- **Audit & Compliance** – Build detailed, checklist‑based audit plans; assign each checklist item to different staff members.
- **Reporting & Insights** – Real‑time dashboards with quick stats, alerts, and exportable CSV reports.
- **Bulk Import (Under Development)** – Import clients and tasks via CSV to accelerate onboarding.

### Why It Helps Finance Professionals
- **Instant firm overview** – Dashboard gives a single glance at client counts, upcoming tasks, and compliance deadlines.
- **Efficient staff allocation** – Role‑based access ensures partners see everything, managers manage assigned clients/staff, and staff see only their own tasks.
- **Compliance assurance** – Automated period selection and audit plan generation keep you ahead of regulatory deadlines.
- **Scalable & Secure** – Built on Supabase for secure data storage and authentication.
